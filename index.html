Mushroom Dishes

Button mushrooms, also known as white mushrooms or Agaricus bisporus, are one of the most versatile mushrooms and can be used in a wide variety of delicious dishes. Here are some popular types of button mushroom meals:

  • Creamy Mushroom Soup

    A classic and comforting soup made with sautéed button mushrooms, onions, garlic, thyme, and cream or milk.

  • Sautéed Button Mushrooms

    A simple side dish featuring sliced button mushrooms sautéed in butter or oil with garlic, herbs, and a splash of white wine.

  • Roasted Mushrooms

    Roasted mushrooms are a flavorful dish that brings out the earthy, nutty taste of the mushrooms while giving them a crispy texture.

  • Stuffed Mushrooms

    Stuffed mushrooms are a staple Thanksgiving appetizer on our holiday menu: they not only up the fancy factor, but they're oh so easy to make.

  • Chicken Mushroom Alfredo

    Combine sautéed button mushrooms with cooked chicken, Alfredo sauce, and pasta to make a rich and satisfying dish.

  • Mushroom Risotto

    Button mushrooms are a great addition to a classic creamy mushroom risotto. Sauté them with onions and Arborio rice, then slowly add broth until the rice is cooked to perfection.
